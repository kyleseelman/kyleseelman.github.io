About

I'm a 5th Ph.D. student at the University of Maryland, luckily advised by the great Dr. Jordan Boyd-Graber, where my research focused on human-centered natural language processing, including interactive topic modeling and large language models. My work bridges the gap between foundational AI research and real-world, human-focused applications.
In the fall I will be working as a Senior Machine Learning Researcher at Roblox, where I will work on cutting-edge problems in 3D/4D Generative AI, search and recommendation systems, and agentic AI.
